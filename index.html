Eligibility Criteria

Check if you meet the requirements for MBBS admission in Nepal

Academic Requirements

  • 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum 50% marks in PCB (45% for SC/ST/OBC)
  • Maximum age limit: 25 years (30 for SC/ST/OBC)
  • NEET qualification mandatory

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • NEET Score Card
  • Valid Passport
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Character Certificate
  • Medical Fitness Certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about MBBS in Nepal

Is NEET required for MBBS admission in Nepal?

Yes, NEET qualification is mandatory for Indian students to pursue MBBS in Nepal. You need to clear NEET to be eligible for admission in NMC recognized medical colleges in Nepal.

Are medical degrees from Nepal valid in India?

Yes, medical degrees from NMC recognized colleges in Nepal are valid in India. Graduates can appear for FMGE (Foreign Medical Graduate Examination) to practice in India.

What is the total duration of MBBS in Nepal?

The MBBS course duration in Nepal is 5.5 years, which includes 4.5 years of academic study and 1 year of mandatory internship.

What are the living expenses for Indian students in Nepal?

The average living expenses range from ₹18,000 to ₹31,000 per month, including accommodation, food, transportation, and other miscellaneous expenses.

Is the medium of instruction English in Nepal medical colleges?

Yes, all NMC recognized medical colleges in Nepal offer MBBS courses in English medium, making it comfortable for Indian students.

What is the admission process for MBBS in Nepal?

The admission process involves document verification, NEET score evaluation, college selection, visa processing, and final admission confirmation. We provide complete guidance throughout the process.
