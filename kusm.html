About Kathmandu University School of Medical Science, Nepal

Leading Medical Education Institution

Established in 1994, Kathmandu University School of Medical Science, Nepal has been at the forefront of medical education

Excellence in Medical Education since 1994

Kathmandu University School of Medical Science, Nepal, located in Dhulikhel, Nepal, is one of the premier medical institutions in Nepal. Established with a vision to provide world-class medical education, Kathmandu University School of Medical Science has been consistently ranked among the top medical colleges in the country.
